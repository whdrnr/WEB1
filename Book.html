The World Wide Web(abbreviated WWW or the Web) is an infromation space where documents and other web resurces are identified by Uniform Resource Locators(URLs), interlinked by hypertext links, and accessible via the internet[1] English scientist Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989, He wrote the frist web browser in 1990 while employed at CERN in Switzerland[2][3] The browser was released outside CERN in 1991, frist to other research institutions starting in January 1991 and to the general public on the Internet in August 1991
